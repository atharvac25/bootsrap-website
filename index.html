Our Vision

At Niti Foundation and Caring Center, our mission is to create a welcoming and supportive home for the elderly. Our goal is to provide comprehensive care, including rehabilitation services, that help our residents regain their health, happiness, and peace of mind. We strive to empower our elderly community members and give them the opportunity to live fulfilling lives. We are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve, and we welcome you to join us in this mission.

About us

The Niti Foundation is dedicated to safeguarding the wellbeing of current members and potential residents. To achieve this, it is crucial to establish a clear and appropriate process for welcoming new residents. Your contributions, no matter how small, will make a significant impact.
